About Us

Will of God Bible Ministry is a Christ-centered, Spirit-led ministry called to reveal the will of God to His people. Our assignment is to bring healing and deliverance, preach the gospel with boldness, and teach the uncompromised Word of God so that lives are transformed and disciples are raised to impact the world.

We are a ministry founded on prayer, faith, and love, believing that Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever. Through evangelism, healing, and teaching, we strive to lead people into a personal relationship with God and equip them to live victoriously.

Our Mission

  • Preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ with boldness and love.
  • Bringing healing and deliverance to the broken, oppressed, and hurting.
  • Teaching and equipping believers with the Word of God for victorious living.
  • Empowering disciples to serve, evangelize, and spread the Kingdom of God everywhere.

Our Vision

To reveal the will of God by transforming lives through healing, deliverance, and the uncompromised Word of God, raising disciples who impact communities and nations for Christ.

Our Core Values

Love

Demonstrating God’s love in all we do, serving with compassion and humility.

Holiness

Living to please God by walking in righteousness and purity.

Faith

Trusting God fully and believing in the power of His Word and promises.

Prayer

Depending on God through prayer, intercession, and fasting.

Integrity

Walking in honesty, transparency, and accountability.

Service

Serving God and others with excellence, generosity, and selflessness.

Evangelism

Reaching the lost and making disciples of all nations.

Healing & Deliverance

Believing in God’s power to heal, restore, and set people free through Jesus Christ.
