About Us

Will of God Bible Ministry is a Christ-centered, Spirit-led ministry called to reveal the will of God to His people. Our assignment is to bring healing and deliverance, preach the gospel boldly, and teach the uncompromised Word of God.

We are founded on prayer, faith, and love, believing that Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever. Through evangelism, healing, and teaching, we strive to lead people into a personal relationship with God.

Our Mission

  • Preaching the gospel with love and boldness.
  • Bringing healing and deliverance to the hurting.
  • Teaching and equipping believers for victorious living.
  • Empowering disciples to serve and spread God’s Kingdom.

Our Vision

To reveal the will of God by transforming lives through healing, deliverance, and the uncompromised Word of God, raising disciples who impact communities and nations for Christ.

Core Values

  • Love – Serving with compassion and humility.
  • Holiness – Walking in righteousness and purity.
  • Faith – Trusting God fully.
  • Prayer – Depending on God through intercession and fasting.
  • Integrity – Honesty and accountability.
  • Service – Excellence and generosity in serving others.
  • Evangelism – Reaching the lost for Christ.
  • Healing & Deliverance – Believing in God’s power to restore.
